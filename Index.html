CUP CAKE QUICK GUIDE

Learn how to make fluffy vanilla cupcakes with our easy step by step instructions. It doesn't take long to rustle up a batch of these delicious cup cakes and less time to eat them so lets get started. You'll only need a few ingredients.

What you'll need

This recipe is simple yet tasty, you'll only need a few ingredients:

  • A half cup of milk
  • 200g Plain flour
  • 200g Granulated sugar
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 1Tblsp Vanilla extract

Your easy step by step guide

Servings.. 8 Cook time.. 20 mins

  1. Sieve 200g of plain flour in to a mixing bowl
  2. Sieve 200g of granulated sugar in to the bowl
  3. Add 200g of butter cut into small cubes
  4. Add 2 eggs
  5. Add half a cup of milk
  6. Add 1 tblsp of vanila extract and mix until smooth with no lumps
